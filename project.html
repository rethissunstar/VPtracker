Our Mission

40K War Tools is designed to be a project to make it easier for wargamers everywhere. It started with us noticing that the VP was a changing value dependent on some of the objectives and location. In some cases we were able to use an easy conversion of 3 or 5 VP will equal one but some of the missions began having 6 vp or 1vp per thing. Keeping track of this stuff for 9th has become a bit arduous. As the codexes are not all out for 10th we are still playing 9th edition. Also it would be nice to see the rules for at least a couple of armies before I begin creating databases.

So we started with this VP tracker. Hoping you will support us on our mission. Constructive feedback always welcome.
